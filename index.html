About me

Hello! I’m Awolesi Olamide Michael, a 23-year-old front-end developer in the making, fueled by curiosity, creativity, and an unshakable drive to build things that live on the web. I began my journey in tech with a deep hunger to understand how the digital world works—and how I could become a creator in it. With a solid grip on HTML and CSS, and an active exploration of JavaScript, I’ve started turning static concepts into interactive experiences. I’m not just learning code—I’m learning how to think like a developer, how to solve problems, and how to build things that people actually enjoy using. My passion lies in crafting clean, modern, and responsive designs that not only look amazing but also work seamlessly across all devices. I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics—it's about user experience, accessibility, and performance. Every line of code I write is a step forward, and every project is a new opportunity to learn, grow, and refine my craft. I’m currently working on real-world projects that challenge me to think deeper, push boundaries, and apply everything I’m learning. From landing pages to full portfolio sites, I’m building up a strong base of work while expanding my knowledge into JavaScript functionality, animations, and eventually frameworks like React. But this is more than just a career path for me—it’s a calling. I see front-end development as a blend of art and engineering, and I’m here to master both sides. I want to build interfaces that feel alive, solve real problems, and inspire users.
